About us

CHOZZ

Chozz is an eco-friendly hygiene product that utilizes palm oil as its main ingredient. We aim to make people aware regarding the dangers of inorganic products and the importance of preserving the environment.

Vision Statement

Minimize the use of inorganic detergents among the community to create a sustainable clean and healthy environment.

Mission Statement

Pioneering and Supporting the use of environmentally friendly household products to raise awareness of the importance of environmental conservation by promoting the manufacture and use of safe, effective, and environmentally friendly household hygiene products in support of achieving the 6th and 12th Sustainable Development Goals: Clean Water and Sanitation, and Responsible Consumption and Production.
